[emmet doc](https://docs.emmet.io/cheat-sheet/)

- Child:  >
- Sibling: +
- Climb-up: ^
- Grouping: ()
- Multiplication: *
- Item numbering: $
- id / class attributes:  #   .
- Custom attributes: [ ]
- Text: {}
- Implicit tag names:
  - .class
  - em>.class
  - ul>.class
